The Perfect Cookie

Description

The perfect Peanut Butter Cookies from Chef John in under 30min

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  1. Step 1: Beat butter, white sugar, brown sugar, peanut butter, salt, baking powder, and baking soda in a stand mixer until smooth.
  2. Step 2: Scrape down the sides of the bowl and beater with a spatula. Add egg and mix for about 1 minute.
  3. Step 3: Slowly mix in flour with the mixer on low. Scrape down the sides of the bowl and beater. Mix on medium until combined, about 30 seconds.
  4. Step 4: Form dough into a ball, cover with plastic and refrigerate for 2-3 hours.
  5. Step 5: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. Step 6: Portion out cookie dough in equal-sized balls on a baking sheet covered with a silicone baking mat. Use damp hands to roll dough into smooth balls. Stamp each ball with a fork in a criss-cross pattern.
  7. Step 7: Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes until golden.
  8. Step 8: Leave on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
  9. Enkoy!
